Frequently Asked Questions

What kind of music does NARIEL make?

NARIEL produces dark dubstep and industrial bass music in the 140–150 BPM range. Tracks are built around minimalism, low-end pressure, and spatial movement rather than dense layering.

How much does ghost production cost?

Ghost production is €350 per track. That includes a full, release-ready track tailored to the artist or media project brief.

How much does mixing and mastering cost?

Mixing and mastering is €100 per track. The service is focused on bass music and tuned for low-end clarity and streaming platform loudness standards.

How long does a ghost production project take?

Turnaround depends on project scope, but most tracks are delivered within 2–3 weeks. Rush delivery can be arranged — contact via the website to discuss timelines.

What makes NARIEL different from other dubstep producers?

NARIEL works with restraint rather than density — silence and negative space are used as active compositional tools. Dolby Atmos is built into the production process from the start, not added at the mastering stage.

What artists sound similar to NARIEL?

NARIEL fits alongside Skrillex, ISOxo, Amon Tobin, Noisia, and Nitepunk — particularly the darker and more experimental side of those catalogues.

Where can I listen to NARIEL's music?

NARIEL's music is available on Spotify, SoundCloud, YouTube, and Bandcamp. The track KENOPSIA is a good starting point — it's available on all platforms.

Do you offer custom sound design or preset packs?

Yes. Custom presets, bass sounds, and FX are available for €75 per project. These are built to spec for bass music producers working in dubstep, trap, and industrial styles.

Why does NARIEL use Dolby Atmos?

Spatial audio is treated as a compositional element, not a mastering add-on. It allows silence and movement to carry the same weight as sound — which is central to the NARIEL sound design approach.

How do I hire NARIEL for production or mixing?

Contact directly via nariel-wav.carrd.co. Include your project brief, reference tracks, and deadline and you'll get a response with availability and pricing.
